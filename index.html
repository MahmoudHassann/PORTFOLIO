    Why are interceptors important?

    Basically, an interceptor is like the middleman between your app and the API. It catches your request right before it goes out, and that's when you can sneak in those headers. Imagine it like stopping a delivery guy right before he leaves, just to add in that extra special instruction – "Oh, by the way, add *Authorization* or some cool *API keys*."

    So, why are interceptors important? Well, instead of adding headers manually in every single request (which is boring and a bit of a pain), you just set it up once in the interceptor, and it handles everything for you. Less hassle, more efficiency – basically, you do it once and chill!

    In short, interceptors are like your helpful little assistant – they save you time, make your code cleaner, and help keep everything running smoothly.
